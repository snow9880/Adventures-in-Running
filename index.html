Running, Jelly Beans, and Life in Between

About Me

I grew up in Colorado Springs, Colorado where I learned to love the outdoors and running. I started running in high school and walked on cross-country team at the University of Northern Colorado. After a few stress fractures, I gave up cross-country to join the Athletic Training program. Athletic Training has provided many opportunities to travel and explore the world around me. From Indiana to China to Guatemala to Ohio, the advenutres continue. After moving to Ohio, I met my fiance, Parker. With the wedding coming, the adventures continue. My free time includes running, knitting, cooking, and learning to program. I love running marathons and half-marathons, but occasionally will run other distances. I also have a secret love of Jelly Beans and almost anything sweet. On the weekend, you can find Parker and I in our favorite local coffe shop.
