BOSU Hit Workout

December 15, 2016

With athletic training, I have always been fascinated by what you can do with a BOSU(both sides used). Recently, I came across the BOSU blog and have been dying to try the new workouts. One of the posts includes doing a BOSU HIIT workout. Yesterday, when it was too cold outside to run, I opted to stay inside and complete one of the hiit workouts. I completed each exercise for 45 seconds and then rested for 15 seconds before moving on to the next exercise. In all, it took me about 25 minutes to complete the hiit program. By the end, I was exhausted. Every single jump reminded me how tired my legs were. It was a great workout! But, my calves are tight today, so I'll find a different workout to complete today.

Bosu HIIT Exercises

  • Burpee with overhead press of the BOSU
  • Alternating lunge holding BOSU and rotating
  • Squat crunch on BOSU
  • Explosive side-to-sides
  • Side plank thread the needle
  • Plank BOSU jacks

Cooking Adventures

Beet Chips

January 4, 2017

Parker and I recently found these amazing beet chips in the grocery store! In an effort to try and make them ourselves, we branched out and tried a new recipe. Sadly, I think it was easier to buy the chips from the store, but it was a fun adventure to try them.

Ingredients

  • A bunch of beets
  • 1Tbsp Olive Oil
  • Sprinkle of Oregano
  • Sprinkle of Thyme
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Sprinkle of Pepper

Directions

  1. Spread olive oil over 2 cookie sheets
  2. Place cookie sheets in oven and preheat to 400 degrees
  3. Meanwhile, cut beets with a mandoline to about 1" thickness
  4. Place beets in a bowl and mix with olive oil, thyme, salt, pepper, and oregano
  5. Once the oven is preheated, take out the cookie sheets and place one layer of beets on each sheet
  6. Place sheets in oven and cook for approximately 8-10 min, rotating the sheets once
  7. Remove the cookie sheets and turn the beets over, then place back in the oven
  8. Cook for about 6-10 more minutes, until the beets have browned. Make sure not to burn the beets
  9. Remove and Enjoy!
