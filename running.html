BOSU Hit Workout

December 15, 2016

With athletic training, I have always been fascinated by what you can do with a BOSU(both sides used). Recently, I came across the BOSU blog and have been dying to try the new workouts. One of the posts includes doing a BOSU HIIT workout. Yesterday, when it was too cold outside to run, I opted to stay inside and complete one of the hiit workouts. I completed each exercise for 45 seconds and then rested for 15 seconds before moving on to the next exercise. In all, it took me about 25 minutes to complete the hiit program. By the end, I was exhausted. Every single jump reminded me how tired my legs were. It was a great workout! But, my calves are tight today, so I'll find a different workout to complete today.

Bosu HIIT Exercises

  • Burpee with overhead press of the BOSU
  • Alternating lunge holding BOSU and rotating
  • Squat crunch on BOSU
  • Explosive side-to-sides
  • Side plank thread the needle
  • Plank BOSU jacks
